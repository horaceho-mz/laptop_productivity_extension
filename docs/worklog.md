# 📝 In-Depth Worklog

## 🔍 V1 Overview (24/05/2026)

### ⚙️ Functions
1. **Paper Holder:** Keeps reference documents in place.
2. **Laptop Clip:** Snaps directly onto the laptop screen.
3. **SSD Holder:** Secures an external solid-state drive.
4. **Hub Holder:** Holds a USB port hub (3 ports) and a LAN cable-to-USB-C adapter.
  
### ⚠️ Issues
1. **Unstable Clipping:** The clipping mechanism is inconsistent and can occasionally release, causing the entire extension to tip backward.
2. **Long term wear&tear effect:** The mechanism that holds the adapter bends the wire and slightly sepeates the part that is inserted into the laptop from the wire.

## 🚲 V2 Overview (01/06/2026)

### ⚙️ New Functions
*(For older functions, please refer to previous versions of the extension.)*

1. **Book Holder:** Supports larger reading materials.
2. **Ice Pack Cooling:** Provides active cooling for the laptop chassis.
3. **Angled Base:** Tilts the setup for a different working posture.
4. **Triangular Side Support Legs:** Adds lateral stability to the frame.
5. **Weekly Task Display Board:** Keeps a physical track of weekly goals.

### 📈 Improvements over V1
1. **Secure Attachment:** Features a much stronger and more consistent clip mechanism onto the laptop.
2. **Heat Reduction:** Achieves faster CPU speeds thanks to the integrated ice pack cooling function.

### ⚠️ Issues
1. **Limited Screen Angle:** The tradeoff for the stronger clipping mechanism is that the laptop screen can only open to a fixed angle, reducing overall ergonomics.
2. **Portability:** Due to the increased size and weight, the extension is harder to transport without completely detaching the cooling and angled support structures.
3. **Long term wear&tear effect:** The mechanism that holds the adapter bends the wire and slightly sepeates the part that is inserted into the laptop from the wire.
